# 🔥 Three Wins

**Three things. Every day. That's it.**

A daily journal that asks for just three wins. Build a streak. Watch the flame grow. No accounts, no cloud, no complexity.

## Features

- **3 wins per day** — focused constraint that builds the habit
- **Streak flame** — CSS-animated fire with 5 levels (1-day ember → 365-day inferno)
- **Milestones** — celebrations at 7, 14, 30, 60, 100, 365 days
- **Weekly digest** — Sunday summary of all your wins
- **Share streak card** — PNG card for social media
- **Works offline** — PWA, everything in localStorage
- **No account needed** — your data stays on your device

## How it works

1. Open the app
2. Write three good things from your day
3. Hit Done
4. Come back tomorrow — watch your flame grow
